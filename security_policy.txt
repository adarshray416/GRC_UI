Information Security Policy

Title: BABCOM Information Security Policy
Version: 3.1
Approved by: Alice Smith, CISO
Approval Date: 2024-01-10
Review Date: 2026-01-10
Scope: All employees, contractors, and third-party vendors who access BABCOM systems and data.
Classification: Confidential

1. Purpose
This policy establishes the framework for managing information security risks across BABCOM operations in line with ISO 27001:2022 and GDPR Article 32.

2. Roles and Responsibilities
The CISO is responsible for maintaining this policy. Department heads are responsible for ensuring their teams comply.

3. Acceptable Use
All users must use BABCOM systems only for authorised business purposes. Personal use is prohibited.

4. Data Classification
All data must be classified as Public, Internal, Confidential, or Restricted.

5. Review
This policy is reviewed annually or following significant changes to the business or regulatory environment.
